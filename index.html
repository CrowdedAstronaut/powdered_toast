The King of Sunrise Flavor

French toast is a breakfast dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. Powdered toast takes that classic and brings it to the next level. A plane of flavor not known until the 1860's when an American Baker by the name of Ren McStimpy accidentally spilled confectioners' sugar (today known as powdered sugar) on his freshly cooked french toast.
